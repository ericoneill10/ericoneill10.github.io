Flippity Flappity – Mechatronic Fish

Project Idea
The goal was to create a fun, interactive fish toy that would respond to stimuli with various “emotions”.
Team Members: Ryan Feng, Kevin Tian
My Responsibilities
Programming a finite state machine on an mbed FDRM-KL25Z microcontroller, sensor and actuator control (servos, LED strips, capacitive touch sensors).
